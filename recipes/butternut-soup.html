Spicy Butternut Squash Soup

Description:

As cold weather descends, there's nothing better than a hearty bunnternut squash soup. Better yet-- a spicy butternut squash soup with coconut cream. This soup pairs well with a grilled cheese sandwhich or a toasted baguette. Serve it as an appetizer or as a main dish.

This recipe serves two people and takes about 1 hour and 30 minutes to cook. It can take longer depending on your oven.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash, halved
  • 1/2 can of full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 garlic head (or two, I won't tell you what to do)
  • 2 thai chili peppers (optional, depends on preferences)
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 1/2 cup of vegetable broth or chicken broth (add more if you want it less thick)
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cilantro or parsley (for garnish)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 380F.
  2. Prepare all listed vegetables and place on the baking dish.
  3. Add olive oil and seasonings.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until vegetables are golden and soft inside.
  5. Place everything in a pot and add cocnut milk and broth. Blend with emulsifier.
  6. Adjust seasonings to taste, garnish and serve.
